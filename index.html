How to Play

01

Move Diagonally

Pieces move one square diagonally forward. Kings can move in any diagonal direction.

02

Capture Opponents

Jump over an enemy piece to capture it. Captures are mandatory when available.

03

Become a King

Reach the opposite back row to crown your piece. Kings move freely in all diagonal directions.

04

Win the Game

Capture all of your opponent's pieces, or leave them with no legal moves remaining.
