CDL Checkers

A clean, modern take on the classic board game — built to be fast, fair, and fun whether you're playing solo or challenging a friend.

Who made this?

CDL Checkers is a project by CDL — a simple site built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no trackers, no bloat. Just a good game.

How does the AI work?

The computer opponent uses a technique called minimax with alpha-beta pruning. In plain English: before every move, the AI mentally plays out several turns ahead — imagining all the ways the game could unfold — and picks the move that leads to the best possible outcome for itself.

It looks ahead up to 5 moves, which makes it a solid challenge without feeling unfair. It also prioritises capturing your pieces over passive moves, so it will always take a jump if it sees one.

The short pause you see before it moves is intentional — it makes the game feel more natural, like playing against a real person who's actually thinking.

Can I play on mobile?

Yes. The board and layout are fully responsive and work on any modern browser, phone or desktop.

Is there any data collected?

No. The game runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. No accounts, no cookies, no tracking.
